Tuckr is a human-first cobotic assistant designed to eliminate the severe physical strain of hotel bed-making. Approaching this challenge through a UX lens, the project establishes a seamless parallel workflow, empowering housekeepers to clean while the device autonomously handles the heavy lifting and perimeter tucking.
